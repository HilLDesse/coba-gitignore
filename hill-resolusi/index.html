Unit 1: Previewing & Predicting

Previewing gives you a general idea about the book and its content. [cite: 12]
Pratinjau (previewing) memberi Anda gambaran umum tentang buku beserta isinya. [cite: 12]

Parts of a Book to Preview

1. The Front Cover usually contains the TITLE, name of the AUTHOR/S, name of Publisher, and ILLUSTRATIONS. [cite: 15]
1. Sampul Depan biasanya memuat JUDUL, nama PENULIS, nama Penerbit, dan ILUSTRASI. [cite: 15]
2. The Inside Cover: Check the date of publication when you preview, if a book is unrevised for a relatively long time, the content may be outdated. [cite: 18]
2. Sampul Dalam: Periksa tanggal publikasi saat Anda meninjau, jika sebuah buku tidak direvisi untuk waktu yang cukup lama, isinya mungkin sudah usang/kedaluwarsa. [cite: 18]
3. Table of Contents: This is the skeletal outline of the content in the book. [cite: 21]
3. Daftar Isi: Ini adalah kerangka dasar (garis besar) dari isi di dalam buku. [cite: 21]
4. Introduction/Preface: A quick check on this information will give you a general idea of what the writer/s plan the book. [cite: 20]
4. Kata Pengantar: Pengecekan cepat pada bagian ini akan memberi Anda gambaran umum tentang apa yang direncanakan penulis untuk buku tersebut. [cite: 20]

Unit 2: Vocabulary in Context

One of the most fundamental vocabulary skills is how to use context to determine meaning. [cite: 431] If you're in a testing situation and you are not allowed to use a dictionary, you must rely on the context clues in the sentence. [cite: 433]
Salah satu keterampilan kosakata paling mendasar adalah bagaimana menggunakan konteks untuk menentukan makna. [cite: 431] Jika Anda berada dalam situasi ujian dan tidak diizinkan memakai kamus, Anda harus mengandalkan petunjuk konteks di dalam kalimat. [cite: 433]

Types of Clues & Word Nuances

Connotation: At a minimum, the connotations of the surrounding words will usually tell you whether the vocabulary word is positive or negative. [cite: 488]
Konotasi: Setidaknya, konotasi dari kata-kata di sekitarnya biasanya akan memberi tahu Anda apakah kata kosakata tersebut bermakna positif atau negatif. [cite: 488]
Homophones: Homophones sound the same but are spelled differently and have different meanings. [cite: 727]
Homofon: Homofon terdengar sama tetapi dieja secara berbeda dan memiliki arti yang berbeda (contoh: sees, seas, seize). [cite: 727]
Homographs: Homographs are spelled the same but are pronounced differently and have different meanings. [cite: 728]
Homograf: Homograf dieja persis sama tetapi diucapkan secara berbeda dan memiliki makna yang berbeda. [cite: 728]

Unit 3: Contextual References

Writers do not like to use the same words many times. [cite: 2123] Transition markers are words used to link ideas together so that the text is easier to read... they refer to a word, or words, mentioned earlier. [cite: 2126] Their function is to refer backwards. [cite: 2127]
Penulis tidak suka menggunakan kata yang sama berkali-kali. [cite: 2123] Penanda transisi adalah kata-kata yang digunakan untuk menghubungkan ide-ide agar teks lebih mudah dibaca... kata-kata ini merujuk pada kata yang disebutkan sebelumnya. [cite: 2126] Fungsinya adalah untuk merujuk kembali ke belakang. [cite: 2127]

Unit 4: Patterns of Organization

Our brain helps us make sense of the world by sorting information into mental networks. [cite: 2601] These networks are organized in patterns, and we use those patterns to understand and remember. [cite: 2602]
Otak kita membantu kita memahami dunia dengan menyortir informasi ke dalam jaringan mental. [cite: 2601] Jaringan ini diatur dalam pola, dan kita menggunakan pola-pola tersebut untuk memahami dan mengingat. [cite: 2602]

The 6 Patterns (Enam Pola Paragraf)

The six common patterns are: Listing, Sequence, Comparison/Contrast, Cause/Effect, Problem/Solution, and Extended Definition. [cite: 2605]
Enam pola umum tersebut adalah: Pendaftaran, Urutan waktu/proses, Perbandingan/Kontras, Sebab/Akibat, Masalah/Solusi, dan Definisi yang diperluas. [cite: 2605]
Pattern Explanation (Penjelasan) Signal Words (Kata Sinyal)
1. Listing Menyajikan ide utama diikuti daftar. For example, for instance, first, second, another, also, besides, in addition. [cite: 2606]
2. Sequence The writer explains the main idea with a series of events or steps in a process that follow one after the other in time order. [cite: 2615] First, second, then, next, after, while, since, soon, finally, in 1965, later, over time. [cite: 2617]

9 Parts of Speech (9 Kelas Kata)

There are 9 parts of speech: 1. verbs 2. nouns 3. adjectives 4. adverbs 5. pronouns 6. conjunctions 7. Articles/Determiner 8. Prepositions 9. Interjection. [cite: 3257]
Terdapat 9 jenis kata penyusun kalimat: 1. kata kerja 2. kata benda 3. kata sifat 4. kata keterangan 5. kata ganti 6. kata hubung 7. Sandang kata 8. Kata depan 9. Kata seru. [cite: 3257]

Types of Nouns (Jenis-Jenis Kata Benda)

  • Common & Proper Nouns: A noun that names a particular person, place, or thing is a proper noun (e.g., Indonesia). [cite: 3262] (Kata benda khusus ditulis dengan huruf kapital).
  • Countable Nouns: Can name people, places, and things that you can count. [cite: 3265] (Bisa dihitung dengan angka).
  • Uncountable Nouns: Name things that you cannot count (e.g., oxygen, sunshine). [cite: 3266] (Tidak bisa dihitung secara individual).
  • Gerunds: Words that end in -ing can be nouns. These nouns are called gerunds. [cite: 3269] (Kata kerja berakhiran -ing yang mengambil fungsi sebagai kata benda).

8 Kinds of Pronouns [cite: 3274-3281]

Terdapat 8 jenis spesifik *pronoun*: Personal (I, you), Possessive (My, mine), Demonstrative (This, those), Interrogative (Who?, What?), Relative (who, which, that), Indefinite (Everyone, someone), Reflexive (Myself, himself), dan Reciprocal (each other).
